# Privacy Policy for LifePods

**Last updated: February 4, 2026**

## Introduction

LifePods ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
- We collect and use your device's location data to provide personalized podcast recommendations based on your commute
- Location data is used to detect commute patterns and estimate travel time
- Location data is processed locally on your device and is not shared with third parties

### Usage Information
- App usage statistics and preferences
- Podcast listening history and preferences
- User settings and customization choices

### Account Information (if applicable)
- Email address (if you choose to create an account)
- Display name and profile information (if provided)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide personalized podcast recommendations
- Calculate commute duration and suggest appropriate content
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Send notifications about new content (with your permission)

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely on your device
- We implement industry-standard security measures
- Location data is not stored on external servers
- We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties

## Third-Party Services

LifePods uses the following third-party services:
- **Google Maps API**: For location services and mapping functionality
- **Audio streaming services**: For podcast content delivery

These services have their own privacy policies and terms of use.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Delete your account and associated data
- Opt-out of location tracking (note: this will limit app functionality)
- Disable notifications at any time

## Children's Privacy

LifePods is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

## Data Retention

- User preferences: Retained until account deletion
- Location data: Processed in real-time, not permanently stored
- Usage statistics: Aggregated and anonymized after 90 days

## International Users

If you are accessing LifePods from outside the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States.
