What is ARcheos?

ARcheos is Born

ARcheos is a mobile app in Android/iOS which aims to merge ancient philosophy with modern technology, offering an unparalleled AR/AI experience in Athens, Greece.

State of the Art Tech

Combining artificial intelligence, augmented reality with location awareness (VPS), and natural language understanding with the richness of ancient Greek philosophy, ARcheos offers users an immersive and educational experience. Users encounter virtual historical figures at landmark locations in Athens who can engage in meaningful conversations, possess memory and personality, and participate in dynamic philosophical discussions with them.
